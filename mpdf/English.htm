Literature

 

5.2a Literary Elements—Demonstrate knowledge of literary elements and techniques and how they affect the development of a literary work. a. Compare and contrast plots, settings, or characters presented by different authors and the same author of multiple texts.

        <<17>>

5.2b Literary Elements—Demonstrate knowledge of literary elements and techniques and how they affect the development of a literary work. b. Recognize themes that occur across literary works. Example: Read Yoko by Rosemary Wells and You Are Special by Max Lucado. Discuss the theme of “everyone is unique” that occurs in both stories.

        <<18>>

5.3 Figurative Language and Sound Devices—The student will identify figurative language and sound devices in writing and how they affect the development of a literary work. Example: Identify and discuss how certain words and rhythmic patterns can be used in a selection to imitate sounds (e.g., rhythm, rhyme, alliteration).
